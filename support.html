How Donations Are Used

Bullish And Foolish is a personal project, not a big company. Donations help keep it running, improve the ratings, and add new features — without paywalls or ads.

Where your support goes

  • Data access: paying for APIs and data feeds used to calculate ratings.
  • Hosting & infrastructure: keeping the site fast, stable and online.
  • Development time: designing new features, improving the rating logic and fixing issues.
  • Future tools: watchlists, portfolio health score, better screeners and compare tools.

Why support matters

I want Bullish And Foolish to stay independent, simple and free to use.

Instead of covering the site in ads or pushing trading platforms, I’d rather be honest: if this project helps you, and you’re in a position to support it, your contribution directly funds its growth.
