Our Methodology

Transparency is the foundation of Fundamental Intelligence. We strip away market sentiment to focus on what actually matters: the numbers.

01

Direct EDGAR Ingestion

We fetch data directly from the SEC's EDGAR database and parse 10-K, 10-Q, and 8-K filings to build standardized financial statements. Updates follow the cadence of new filings and are cached for performance.

02

Sector-Aware Scoring

A Biotech company shouldn't be rated like a Retailer. Our engine applies custom benchmarks for Profitability, Growth, and Solvency based on the company's industry SIC code. We adjust for R&D intensity, inventory cycles, and capital structures.

03

Filing Intelligence

Beyond the numbers, we scan filing text for signals such as going concern language, dilution risk, or auditor changes that basic ratios can miss.

04

The Bullish & Foolish Rating

Results are normalized into a 0-100 Quality Score and summarized into tiers like Elite Compounder, Investment Grade, and High Risk to make the takeaway instantly clear.
