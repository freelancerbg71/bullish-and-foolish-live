About Bullish And Foolish

Investing shouldn't feel like deciphering an income statement in Latin. Bullish And Foolish cuts through the noise, condenses the mountain of financial data, and shows you the signal - fast.

The markets are chaotic, emotional, euphoric and brutal. This tool doesn't tell you what to buy - instead, it helps you understand why something may look promising or questionable.

No hype. No paid pumps. No financial advice - just perspective.

Why I Built It

Most stock platforms drown you in ratios, charts and jargon.

When I started investing, every analyst page felt like a wall of data: P/E, EV/EBITDA, FCF, margins, short interest… Useful, but overwhelming if you're not living in spreadsheets 8 hours a day.

So I built a system that flips the view:

  • ➡ Instead of data first, you get clarity first
  • ➡ It acts as an EDGAR Translator — turning raw filings into plain English
  • ➡ Instead of experts talking down to you, you get a compass you can understand

Bullish And Foolish was made for people who think independently - not blindly follow hype.

Before

  • 40 ratios, long reports
  • Decision fatigue
  • Signals buried in noise

After

  • 1 score and snapshot
  • Clarity first, data second
  • A compass you can use quickly

"I didn't want another analyst page. I wanted a compass.”

How the Ratings Work

Each rating combines several pillars of a business into a single, easy-to-read score.

📈
Growth
Revenue and earnings trajectory.
💰
Profitability & Margins
Gross, operating, and free cash flow strength.
🏛
Balance Sheet & Debt
Leverage, coverage, and liquidity.
📊
Valuation
Multiples and yield vs. quality.
👥
Shareholder Yield
Buyback and dilution trends.
🧠
Sentiment & Momentum
Price action and stability signals.
📑
Filing Signals
Textual analysis of risks and legal proceedings from 10-K/Qs.

What's Next

This is a beta. Here's what I'd like to build next:

  • 📊Expand the stock screener with more filters by rating range and fundamentals.
  • 🧠Strengthen and refine rating metrics for better accuracy and consistency.
  • 📈Portfolio health score to quickly see balance, strength and risk exposure.
  • ⭐Watchlist support so you can track favorite tickers in one place.
